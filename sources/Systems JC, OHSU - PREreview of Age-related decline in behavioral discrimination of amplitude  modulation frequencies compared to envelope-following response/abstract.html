This is a preprint journal club review of "Age-related decline in behavioral discrimination of amplitude modulation frequencies compared to envelope-following responses" by Jesyn Lay, Edward L. Bartlett. The preprint was posted on bioRxiv on Sep 28, 2017 (DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.1101/193268).
