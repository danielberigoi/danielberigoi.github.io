Summary

This paper adds to a current body of research detailing the resistance mechanism triggered by the Pto effector HopQ1 in N. benthamiana; a 2017 paper identified the cognate resistance gene, Roq1 \cite{Schultink2017}, and a very recent publication showed that the CCR-NB-LRR (RNL) NRG1 is a required downstream component to mediate the response \cite{Qi_2018}.
